privacy controls

Cookie Policy

olegotka.es keeps analytics optional. Google Analytics stays off until you accept it, and you can change that choice from the footer at any time.

Current control

Essential language and theme preferences may be stored locally. Analytics cookies are only used after consent.

Essential storage

Some pages store language or theme preferences in local storage. This remembers your interface choice and is not used for advertising or cross-site tracking.

Optional analytics

If you accept analytics cookies, this website loads Google Analytics with measurement ID G-75T821TKQY. It helps us understand page views, approximate geography, device and browser information, referral source, and general site usage so the website can be improved.

Provider Purpose Example cookies
Google Analytics Audience measurement and site improvement after consent. _ga, _ga_75T821TKQY, _gid

Consent and withdrawal

Analytics is disabled by default. When you accept, your choice is saved in local storage. You can change or withdraw your choice through the cookie settings link in the footer. If you reject analytics after accepting, the site asks Google tags to stop analytics storage and removes common Google Analytics cookies for this domain.

Third party information

Google may process analytics data according to its own terms and privacy documentation. Read more in the Google Privacy Policy and Google Analytics privacy controls.

Contact

For privacy or cookie questions, contact hello@olegotka.es.
